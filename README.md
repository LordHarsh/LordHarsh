I spend most of my time making AI systems that actually work in production, not just in notebooks. Currently at **EmendoAI** where I maintain 200+ Lambda functions across 35 CDK stacks for a construction SaaS — the kind of infrastructure number that sounds made up until you open the AWS console.

Before that I built **[Halocard](https://halocard.co)** at Quinence, a crypto-backed Visa card where the entire Web3 layer is invisible to the user. Real card, real KYC, real money. And **[PaddockPath](https://paddockpath.com)**, a cattle management system across Web + iOS + Android with offline-first sync because farms don't have great 4G.

The research side: spent a summer at NIT Trichy extending a published IEEE paper on infant pose estimation. 93.6 mAP. Children's healthcare is a genuinely hard computer vision problem and I liked that it mattered.
